Kapaleeshwarar Temple

About Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Kapaleeshwarar Temple, located in Mylapore, Chennai, is one of the most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Built in the Dravidian architectural style, the temple is known for its intricate sculptures, tall gopuram, and rich cultural heritage. It is a significant pilgrimage site and a center of Tamil religious traditions.

Santhome Basilica

About Santhome Basilica

Santhome Basilica, located in Chennai, is a Roman Catholic minor basilica built over the tomb of St. Thomas, one of Jesus' twelve apostles. The neo-Gothic style church, built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and later renovated by the British, is a significant religious site and a major landmark of Chennai.
